Welcome to COPTOTHON 2025

Welcome to COPTOTHON 2025 – Drone Design & Competition organized by the Centre of Excellence (Drone Technology), Dept. of Aeronautical Engineering, SMACE, KARE.

Use this dashboard to register, check-in, and view results. Participants can register once. Admin control is securely handled server-side.

📘 Competition User Manual

  • Round 1 – Presentation Round: Team presents their drone design, concept, and innovation.
  • Round 2 – Assembly & Preliminary Flight Test: Guided rope path flight test to check control & stability.
  • Round 3 – Final Flight Test: Free-flight test showing drone control and performance.

🧰 Materials Provided

  • Micro drone kit – ice sticks, pencil motors, propellers, wires
  • Glue gun, adhesives, soldering kit & tools

🗓️ Event Timeline

  • Oct 29 & 30: Pre-workshop & Training (5 PM – 7 PM)
  • Oct 31 – Nov 1: 30-hour Hackathon – Build, Program & Fly

🎯 Objectives

  • Hands-on drone fabrication and design
  • Encourage innovation, teamwork & problem-solving

🏁 Evaluation Criteria

  • Design & Innovation – 30%
  • Preliminary Flight Test – 30%
  • Final Flight Test – 40%
